vs. Wind Speed (mph):



In this graph, we analyzed the relationship between latitude and wind speed in miles per hour (mph). In general, it seems there is no correlation by latitude and wind speed. However, by looking at the data carefully, general trends seems to appear. First of all, there appears to be a peak in wind speed in cities with latitudes close to (+)~60 and (-)~60. This data appears to be in agreement which what we know about how earth rotation affects wind speed by generating the easterlies and westerlies, for example.
