vs. Temperature (F)

In this analysis, we plotted the relationship between temperature (on y axis) and lattitude (on x axis). Not surprisingly, there is a clear relationship between the two. The closer you are to lattitute zero (equator), the warmer the temperature gets. In addition, the cities with coordinates on the (+) latitude (Northern Hemisphere), the temperature starts getting colder (lower temperature), since the date where these measurements were taken was in early March, is transitioning from winter to spring. By contrast cities with (-) lattitude coordinates (Southern Hemisphere) is fall and is transitioning from summer to winter time. Lastly, the closer the cities are to the equator, the warmer the temperature gets, in agreement with the known tropical weather of these cities.
